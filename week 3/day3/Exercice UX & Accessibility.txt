Accessibility Features

1. Adjustable Game Speed 
Where it appears: In the Pause Menu and Settings Menu before starting the game.
Purpose: Allows players to slow down the game if they have slower reaction times or are still learning the mechanics.

2. High-Contrast Mode
Where it appears: In the Settings Menu, with a toggle option.
Purpose: Changes platform, player, and background colors to strong, contrasting tones to help players with visual impairments clearly see important elements.

Helpful In Game Prompt

Tap or press SPACE to jump. Time your jump before the platform disappears
