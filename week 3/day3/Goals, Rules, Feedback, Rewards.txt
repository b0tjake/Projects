Core Structure

Goal:
Survive as long as possible while jumping between platforms and collecting energy cells.

Rule 1:
If the player falls off a platform, the game ends.

Rule 2:
The game speed gradually increases over time.

Feedback

Success Feedback:
A bright flash effect and a cheerful sound play when the player collects an energy cell or lands a perfect jump.

Failure Feedback:
A short crash animation and a low “fail” sound play when the player falls, followed by the game over screen.

Cosmetic Reward

Reward:
Unlock new robot skins (colors or simple outfits).
