Room 1 –> Teach

Layout:
A straight path of large disappearing platforms with slow fade timers. No enemies or hazards.

Purpose:
Introduce the mechanic safely so the player understands that platforms wont stay forever.

Checkpoint:
At the end of the room.
Reason: Saves time by avoiding repetition of the tutorial section.

Room 2 –> Test

Layout:
Platforms disappeaer faster, and small gaps are introduced. The player must keeps moving to avoid falling.

Purpose:
Test the player's understanding of timing and movement.

Checkpoint:
Midway through the room.
Reason: Reduces frustration if the player fails while learning the timing.

Room 3 –> Twist

Layout:
Disappearing platforms mixed with moving platforms. Some platforms move horizontally while fading out.

Purpose:
Combine mechanics to create a more challenging and dynamic experience.

Checkpoint:
At the start of the room.
Reason: Prevents the player from replaying the earlier, easier rooms after each failure.

Room 4 –> Master

Layout:
A fast-paced sequence of small disappearing and moving platforms with narrow gaps.

Purpose:
Challenge the player to use everything learned in previous rooms.

Checkpoint:
None inside the room, only after completion.
Reason: Maintains tension and makes the final challenge feel rewarding when completed.
