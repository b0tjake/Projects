Questions & Answers

Main Verb:
Jump

Win Condition:
Reach a target score or break your previous score.

Lose Condition:
Miss a platform and fall.

Biggest Issue Discovered:
Players were confused about how far they could jump.

Fix Applied:
Added a fixed jump range rule (player can only jump up to 2 spaces).
