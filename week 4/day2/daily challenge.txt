1) One Major Technological Advancement: The Persistent Online Connectivity (Broadband Internet).
Without high-speed, always on internet, World of Warcraft couldn't have hosted thousands of players in one world, and the social infrastructure of the early 2000s would have stayed limited to small local matches.

2) One Community Trend: Massive Scale Social Interaction. World of Warcraft capitalized on the player's desire to belong to a digital third-place.
It turned gaming from a solitary activity into a persistent community where your reputation and friendships mattered as much as your high score.
